Summer Program

Why you should keep dancing during the summer

At summer intensives & camps, dancers are immersed in a variety of styles, repertoire, and genres such as ballet, modern, contemporary, jazz, and improvisation, expanding their versatility and adaptability. These programs offer more than just extra classes; they are transformative experiences that can significantly impact a dancer’s technical growth. Even one focused summer can be a pivotal moment in a dancer’s journey. At the camps and intensives, dancers will also create lasting memories and meaningful friendships as they grow, learn, and have fun together.
