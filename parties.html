Events & Parties

Get ready for dancing, laughter, and tons of birthday fun!

At Dancing For Joy, we make every party feel magical. Whether you're celebrating a birthday, welcoming a new baby, or planning a girls' night full of music and movement, we have exciting party packages that bring your theme to life with colorful decorations and plenty of time to dance.

Explore our party options below to find the perfect fit for your special occasion.

Ready to book?

Email Ms Heather, our event coordinator, at heatherkurigadfj@gmail.com.

The following applies to all party packages:

Guest Capacity: 35 people.

  • 30 minutes for you to setup before the party
  • Theme of your choice and/or color(s)
  • Themed decorations, including:
  • Decorated Cake/Snack Table
  • Paper Cake Plates
  • Napkins
  • Tableclothes

Pricing for 12 Guests:

  • $100 deposit to hold your rate
  • $125 balance due the day of party
  • $5 per additional guest
  • $50 per additional 30 min.
